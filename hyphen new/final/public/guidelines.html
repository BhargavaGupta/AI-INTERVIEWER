AI Interviewer Exam Guidelines

Before the Exam

  • Arrive Early: Ensure you are online and ready at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time of the exam.
  • Identification: Make sure you have a valid ID ready for verification if required.
  • Technical Setup: Check your internet connection, camera, and microphone to ensure they are working properly.
  • Environment: Choose a quiet, well-lit place for the exam with minimal distractions.

During the Exam

Dos

  • Read Instructions Carefully: Follow all instructions provided by the AI interviewer.
  • Manage Your Time: Allocate time wisely between questions and ensure you answer all parts.
  • Stay Calm: Maintain composure and focus throughout the exam.
  • Ask for Clarification: If you don't understand a question, ask the AI for clarification.

Don'ts

  • Do Not Engage in Distractions: Avoid any activities that could distract you from the exam.
  • Do Not Use Unauthorized Aids: Refrain from using external resources or unauthorized materials.
  • Do Not Speak to Others: Avoid communicating with others during the exam unless necessary for technical issues.
  • Do Not Leave the Exam: Remain in the exam environment until the exam is completed or you are instructed otherwise.

Good luck on your exam!
